Description

In the 1980s, a young boy, Indiana, disappeared in Hawkins. Friends, family and local police are looking for answers, they are involved in a very mystical investigation. Mike, a friend of the missing hero, begins his own investigation. Mike is convinced that he is close to solving the task and that he is waiting to be at the epicenter of a fierce battle.
